About wave to earth

wave to earth is a South Korean indie band formed in 2018, consisting of Kim Daniel (vocals, guitar), Shin Dongkyu (drums), and John Cha (bass). Their name symbolizes their intention to spread waves of emotion through music.

With a cinematic blend of lo-fi, dream pop, and indie rock, wave to earth is known for their emotionally rich soundscapes, poetic lyrics, and mellow vibes. The band gained major attention with their single Seasons and later exploded in popularity across Asia and beyond.

Often writing about love, loneliness, nature, and the passage of time, wave to earth creates music that feels both nostalgic and timeless. Their visuals, sound, and fashion have made them icons of modern youth subculture, especially among international indie fans.
