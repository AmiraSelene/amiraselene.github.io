About Luke Chiang

Luke Chiang is an R&B singer known for his soulful music and captivating vocals. Born in the United States, Luke Chiang has made a significant impact in the music industry with his popular songs like "Shouldn't Be," "May I Ask," "Paragraphs," and "Used to Me." Despite facing a challenging throat condition between 2019 and 2023 that temporarily hindered his ability to sing, Luke Chiang persevered and continued to pursue his passion for music. He released his debut single, "May I Ask," in 2019, marking the beginning of his musical journey.

Blending soul, R&B, and bedroom pop, Luke's sound is characterized by smooth melodies, jazzy chords, and personal storytelling. His breakout songs like May I Ask and Comfort touch on themes of love, vulnerability, and personal growth, resonating deeply with fans around the world.
